Sparsh Premier League – Season 2 Rules

Tournament Overview

  • Men’s Division: 4 league matches per team
  • Women’s Division: 2 league matches per team
  • Kids Division: 2 league matches per team

Match Format

  1. Each match will consist of 8 overs per side (Women’s matches: 9 overs per side). Each player must bowl one over.
  2. Powerplay Over: Fixed at the 3rd over of each innings.
    • Only one fielder is allowed on the boundary; all others must be ahead of the center line.
    • The Magic Ball rule does not apply during the powerplay.
  3. Magic Ball Rule:
    • The first ball of every over is a Magic Ball.
    • Runs scored on the Magic Ball count double.
    • Wickets lost on the Magic Ball result in a deduction of 5 runs.
  4. Bowler Repeat Rule for Short Players:
    • The repeat bowler must belong to the same category as the short player.
    • For wild category short players, the repeat bowler must be from the last category.

Batting Restrictions

  1. Opening Pair Rule:

    A new opening pair must start the innings in every league match. If an opener is repeated, he/she will be declared out, and any runs scored will be deducted from the team total. This rule does not apply in knockout matches.

  2. Byes and Leg Byes: All byes and leg byes, including when no shot is offered, are allowed.

Fielding Restrictions

  1. Normal Overs: Maximum two fielders are allowed on the boundary; all others must remain ahead of the center line.
  2. Powerplay Over: Only one fielder is allowed on the boundary; all others must be ahead of the center line.
    • Only the front net counts as the boundary.
    • Catches taken after the ball bounces off a side net are not out.
    • Catches off the ceiling net are out.

Bowling & Action Rules

  1. Speed Limit Rule:
    • 1st speed ball: Dead ball.
    • 2nd speed ball: No-ball.
    • Applicable only if the batsman appeals and the umpire agrees.
  2. Bowling Action Rule:
    • Over-arm bowling is not allowed.
    • 1st over-arm ball: Dead ball.
    • 2nd over-arm ball: No-ball.
  3. Bowling Box Rule:
    • A box will be marked at the bowling crease.
    • Touching the side or back line = No-ball.
    • Crossing the front line = No-ball.
  4. Side & Keeper Confirmation:
    • The bowler must confirm the bowling side (over/around) and any keeper position change before delivery.
    • Failure to do so will result in a No-ball.
    • Every no-ball is followed by a Free Hit.
    • For stumping or run-out, line touch is considered not out.

Equipment & Dress Code

  1. Compulsory Gear: Team jersey and shoes are mandatory.
  2. Lower Wear: Only track pants are permitted; shorts are not allowed.
  3. Bat Restriction: Fiber bats are not allowed.

Batting & Running

  1. Runner Rule: Only one player may act as a runner for the entire match, regardless of injury or other reasons.

Scoring & Tie-Breakers

  1. Scorer Requirement: One member from the batting team must sit with the scorer during the innings.
  2. Score Discrepancies: Any scoring disputes must be raised immediately during the innings; post-match complaints will not be accepted.
  3. Tie-Breaker Rules:
    • League Matches:
      1. Team with the most sixes.
      2. If still tied, team with the most fours.
      3. If still tied, team with fewer wickets lost.
    • Knockout Matches: Decided by a Super Over.

General Rules

  1. The Umpire’s decision will be final and binding in all circumstances.
  2. The Tournament Committee reserves the right to revise, amend, or interpret any rule or decision as necessary for the betterment of the tournament.
