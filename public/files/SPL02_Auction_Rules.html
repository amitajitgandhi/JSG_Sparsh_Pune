Sparsh Premier League – Auction Rules

General Rules

  1. Virtual Balance: Each team starts with a balance of 1 Crore.
  2. Player Acquisition: Teams must purchase 8 players (except 9 players for Women’s category), respecting minimum and maximum limits per category.
  3. Bidding Process:
    • Bids start at the player’s base price.
    • Maximum bidding time per player: 2 minutes.
    • Only team owners or their representatives can place or raise bids.
  4. Balance Management:
    • If a team bids more than their available balance, a warning is issued.
    • Exceeding the balance incurs a 1 Lakh penalty, and the bid is invalid.
  5. Player Management:
    • Once purchased, players cannot be swapped or changed.
    • Bids must be placed using a baton/bat; verbal bidding is not allowed.
    • Bids cannot be canceled once placed.
  6. Communication Restrictions: Use of any communication devices during the auction is prohibited.
  7. Owner Category: Owners or their blood relatives (if participating) may retain one player only.
  8. Unsold Players:
    • Unsold players will be re-auctioned at their base price.
    • If multiple players remain unsold post re-auction, they will be randomly distributed using a spin wheel at their respective category base price.
    • If only one team is left to purchase the last player, the player will be assigned at the category base price.
  9. Bid Increment Rules:
    • Bids must be in multiples of 1 Lakh.
    • Each bid must increase by at least 1 Lakh.
  10. All-In Bids:
    • If two or more teams go ALL-IN for the same player, allocation will be randomly determined using a spin wheel.

Auctioneer & Committee

  • The auctioneer’s decision is final.
  • The Core Committee (JSG PUNE SPARSH Committee) has the final authority on all matters.

Men’s Division – 11 Teams

Booster System: Teams may buy Boosters to gain extra points.

  • 1 Booster = 10 Lakh points for 1,000 Rupees.
  • Maximum 5 Boosters per team.
  • Boosters can only be applied before a new player is displayed or after the current player is sold. They cannot be used during an ongoing auction.

Player Categories:

  • Icon Players: Base Price: 15 Lacs, Total: 22 Players (2 per team)
  • Platinum Players: Base Price: 7 Lacs, Total: 44 Players (4 per team)
  • Gold Players: Base Price: 3 Lacs, Total: 22 Players (2 per team)

Women’s Division – 4 Teams

Player Categories:

  • Icon Players: Base Price: 15 Lacs, Total: 4 Players (1 per team)
  • Platinum Players: Base Price: 8 Lacs, Total: 8 Players (2 per team)
  • Gold Players: Base Price: 5 Lacs, Total: 16 Players (4 per team)
  • Wildcard Players: Base Price: 3 Lacs, Total: 8 Players (2 per team)

Kids Division – 4 Teams

Player Categories:

  • Icon Players: Base Price: 15 Lacs, Total: 8 Players (2 per team)
  • Platinum Players: Base Price: 8 Lacs, Total: 8 Players (2 per team)
  • Gold Players: Base Price: 5 Lacs, Total: 8 Players (2 per team)
  • Wildcard Players: Base Price: 3 Lacs, Total: 4 Players (1 per team)

Additional Notes

  • Players on the waiting list may replace any team player who backs out; replacements will be arranged by the committee.
  • The committee’s decision regarding player replacements is final; teams are expected to comply.
  • All rights are reserved by the JSG PUNE SPARSH Committee.
